# Founder Panel on Product Hunt

## Tagline

Startup advice grounded in 50 founders' public writing

## Description

Founder Panel turns 20,347 public posts from 50 founders and investors into source-grounded startup advice inside Claude Code. Go one-on-one with a founder synthesis or convene a 2–5 person board, with every substantive claim linked to the original writing.
